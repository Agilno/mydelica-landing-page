Data Policy

 

Protecting your personal information is of utmost importance to us, and we take extreme measures to keep it safe. We are committed to being fully transparent and want to make sure you understand how and where your information is stored, how it’s used and what you can do with it.

Most importantly, you own your data! You may ask us to have your data promptly deleted at any time. All data that you enter and track on the MyDelica App is anonymous and kept on servers in the USA only. All communications between our App on your device and our servers is encrypted. This means that no data ever sits on your device. As well, our entire technology platform is privacy compliant in the U.S. (HIPAA), with the goals of being compliant in Canada (PIPEDA and PHIPA), and EU (GDPR). These are the highest standards for privacy of health information in these jurisdictions.

Our mission is to advance the scientific understanding of psychedelic substances for medical and therapeutic purposes. To that end, we aggregate and anonymize all data for the purpose of studying real-time, evidence based experiences with psychedelic substances in order to learn how psychedelic substances work and to improve treatment for all patients:

● We provide free access to our anonymous information to approved academic researchers & clinicians for non-commercial study and peer-reviewed medical publication; and

● We sell software subscription access to anonymous information and technology tools for researching that information, as well as internally-designed research reports, to industry organizations so that they can better serve you.

● We do NOT gather or sell patient names or purchase information.

We use industry standard “cookies” on our webpages to optimize your experience with our services.

Our company was founded by doctors and scientists, and that’s why patients and science come first to us. If there’s anything further you wish to understand, please see our Privacy Policy https://www.mydelica.com/privacy-policy, our End User License Agreement https://www.mydelica.com/eula and our FAQs https://www.mydelica.com/faq. If there’s still something you’d like to understand better, please reach us at support@mydelica.com.

Thanks for your time and have a great day,

The MyDelica Team
